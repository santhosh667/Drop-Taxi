About Us

Welcome to Chennai Premium Drop Taxi, your trusted travel partner for safe, comfortable, and reliable rides. We specialize in providing one-way and long-distance drop taxi services designed to make your journey smooth and stress-free.

Chennai Premium Drop Taxi is an Inter-city taxi booking facilitator. It is owned and operated by Sanviv Services Private Limited, a company incorporated in India, having its registered office at No 286 GST Road, Chrompet, Chennai -600044. It is involved in Supporting and auxiliary transport activities; activities of travel agencies.

We started this venture out of a personal pain point of having to pay two way charges for a one way drop trip.

Market taxis charge two-ways, usually citing the reason of returning empty.

By harnessing information and communication technologies, we have disrupted the conventional practice.

Since inception in 2014 we have served 2,00,000 Customer, and in the process,have saved close to Rs.28 crores for them

Chennai Premium Drop Taxi indeed saves more than 30% to customers for drop trips.

Roundtrips and Package Trips available at competitive rates.

At Chennai Premium Drop Taxi, we offer a wide range of well-maintained commercial and luxury vehicles to suit every travel need. Whether it’s an airport transfer, business trip, family travel, or special occasion, we ensure a comfortable and hassle-free experience.

Our professional drivers are experienced, courteous, and committed to your safety. We focus on timely pickups, transparent pricing, and customer satisfaction at every step.

With 24/7 service availability, easy booking, and affordable fares, Chennai Premium Drop Taxi aims to redefine convenient travel. Choose us for a reliable ride, exceptional service, and a journey you can trust.
